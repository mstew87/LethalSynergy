About Us

Lethal Synergy was founded in 1998 by Naxon Vek to support a small guild in Everquest. It is an active and large gaming community with over 500 members currently supporting 3 major MMO titles: Final Fantasy XIV, Elder Scrolls Online and Lost Ark
