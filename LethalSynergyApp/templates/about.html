About Us

Lethal Synergy was founded in 1998 by Naxon Vek to support a small guild in Everquest. It is an active and large gaming community with over 500 members currently supporting 3 major MMO titles: Final Fantasy XIV, Elder Scrolls Online and Lost Ark.

Lethal Synergy believes in community first, game second to create a place where people of all backgrounds can come together and ejoy their shared hobby of social online games. Social aspects are not all we focus on. Lethal Synergy has participated in the hardest end game content in every game we have ever had a presence in.
